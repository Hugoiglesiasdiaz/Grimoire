About Grimoire

What is Grimoire?

Grimoire is an intelligent document processing and analysis platform that leverages AI technology to help you extract insights from documents. Whether you need to summarize lengthy reports, search through multiple documents, or analyze content, Grimoire makes it simple and efficient.

Key Features

  • Document Upload: Upload PDF, DOCX, TXT, and DOC files
  • Text Extraction: Automatically extract text from your documents
  • AI Summarization: Generate concise summaries powered by Google Gemini
  • Semantic Search: Search across documents using natural language
  • Database Storage: Keep all your documents organized and searchable

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQLite
  • AI/ML: Google Gemini API
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

How to Use

  1. Go to the Home page
  2. Upload your documents using the upload area
  3. Extract text from your documents
  4. Generate summaries or search through your documents

Privacy & Security

Your documents are stored securely on the server. We do not share your data with third parties except for the Google Gemini API for summarization and analysis features.
